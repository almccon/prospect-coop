A limited-equity housing cooperative in Downtown Bellingham, Washington

Our Mission: The Prospect Street Co-op will build a long-term affordable home together in downtown Bellingham. We will show that a better kind of housing is possible for a sustainable and just society.

Find Out More

Our vision

The Prospect Coop is a new paradigm in community-led development. We are a permanently affordable, multi-generational housing cooperative. We are committed to one another, our community, and the land. We are a home for inspiration, cooperation, and belonging.

Learn why

Our values

We value ecological responsibility, sustainable design, beauty, and permanent affordability.

We are intentionally inclusive.

We welcome diversity.

We understand the necessity of working together, joy, and honoring our differences.

We respect the balance of personal privacy, community life, and involvement in activities beyond co-living.

We are stewards of our shared space, neighborhood, city, and region.

We sustain our common society and environment.

We value conflict resolution that is nonviolent, positive, respectful, and fair.
